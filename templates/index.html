Let \(z \in C\), \(z = r(\cos(\varphi) + i\sin(\varphi))\). There are \(n\) many \(n-th\) roots of \(z\) which are given by the formula: $$\sqrt[n]{z} = \sqrt[n]{r}(\cos(\frac{\varphi + 2k\pi}{n} + i\sin(\frac{\varphi + 2k\pi}{n}))$$ $$k=0,...,n-1$$
